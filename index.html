Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats do you support? expand_more

We currently support PDF, DOCX (Word), TXT, and Markdown files. We are working on adding image-to-text (OCR) support soon.

Is my data private? expand_more

Absolutely. Your files are encrypted at rest and in transit (256-bit SSL). We do not train our public models on your private study data.

Can I export my study plans? expand_more

Yes! You can export your summaries and quizzes to PDF, or export flashcards directly to Anki and Quizlet formats.
