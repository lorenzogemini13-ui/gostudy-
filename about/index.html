We are building the
calculator for your brain.

In 1970, the electronic calculator freed people from manual arithmetic so they could focus on real problem-solving.

GoStudy! is an attempt to do the same for studying.

Summarizing notes, creating flashcards, and organizing material is necessary work, but it is not learning. Learning happens when concepts connect.

GoStudy! is being built to automate the mechanical parts of studying so students can focus on understanding, not formatting.
