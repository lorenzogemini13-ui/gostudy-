Supporting independent learning.

GoStudy! is designed to help students organize their materials, not to do the work for them. A transparent look at our philosophy.

What GoStudy! is (and isn't)

It is an organizer

Students upload their own messy lecture notes, PDFs, and handouts. Our system structures this information into clean summaries and study guides. It encourages students to review material they have already been taught.

It is not a homework generator

We do not write essays from scratch. We do not solve math problems without context. GoStudy! requires the student to provide the source material, ensuring they are engaging with their actual curriculum.

Building better habits

filter_list

Reducing Cognitive Overload

Modern students often face hundreds of pages of reading per week. By identifying the key concepts automatically, we help students focus their energy on understanding the core material rather than just trying to get through the reading list.

psychology

Active Recall over Passive Reading

Scientific research shows that testing yourself (Active Recall) is more effective than re-reading notes. GoStudy! automatically generates practice quizzes from the student's notes, prompting them to test their knowledge immediately.

A typical use case

  1. After Class

    The student uploads a photo of their handwritten history notes or a PDF of a biology chapter.

  2. Processing

    GoStudy! organizes the notes into a structured timeline and creates a set of key definitions.

  3. Review

    The student takes a 10-question generated quiz to verify they understood the lecture.

Common Questions

Is using AI considered cheating?

Using AI to write an essay for you is academic dishonesty. Using AI to organize your own notes, create flashcards, or explain a difficult concept is a study aid, similar to a tutor or a study guide. We encourage responsible use and our tools are built to support retention, not generation.

Is the data private?

Yes. We understand that students upload personal notes and school materials. All data is encrypted, and we do not sell user data to advertisers or third parties.

Is it accurate?

GoStudy! works strictly with the material provided by the student. It does not browse the internet to "invent" facts. However, like any digital tool, it should be used as a companion to the textbook, not a replacement.
