Get Started

A transparent process.

We do not replace studying. We remove the clutter so you can focus on the learning.

upload_file

1. You upload your material

Upload your raw lecture slides (PDF), essays (Word), or even photos of loose handwritten notes.

We support the formats schools actually use. You do not need to format or clean the data beforehand; just provide the source.

sort

2. We identify the concepts

GoStudy! analyzes the document to find the core learning objectives. It separates critical information from filler text.

This is not just summarizing; it is structuring. We map out connections between topics to ensure nothing important is missed during the reduction process.

inventory_2

3. You get a clear plan

Within seconds, you receive a clean, printable summary and a set of Active Recall questions.

Instead of re-reading 50 pages of passive text, you get a direct list of what you need to know, formatted for easy memorization.

school

4. You learn efficiently

Use the generated quizzes for daily 10-minute reviews, or use the memory palace hints for long-term retention.

This fits into your existing routine—whether you are cramming the night before or studying steadily throughout the semester.
